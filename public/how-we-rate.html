📊 How We Rate Car Deals

We give each car a score, kind of like a grade. Here’s what we look at:

  • Price: Cheaper than market = 🌟
  • Mileage: Lower mileage = 👍
  • Year: Newer = 😎
  • Features: Leather seats, sunroof, navigation? Bonus points!
  • Warranty: Certified cars get extra love.

All that adds up to a score out of 100 — higher is better!
