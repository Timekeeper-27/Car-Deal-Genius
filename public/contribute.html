🛠️ How to Add New Car Sites

Want to help? Add new car listing formats!

  1. Open a site with car listings.
  2. Right-click a car title — hit Inspect.
  3. Find a good class name for:
    • Container (whole car block)
    • Title (name of car)
    • Price
    • Image
    • Link

Then Save a New Format or check out our Deal Rating!
